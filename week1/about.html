About Us - ABSOLUTE MASCULINITY

At AB Boxing Club, we're not just a gym; we're a community of fighters and dreamers. Established in 1995, we've been a cornerstone of our neighborhood, instilling discipline, respect, and determination through the art of boxing.

Our Story

From humble beginnings, we fought against the odds, both in the ring and out. Our first ring was a makeshift one, pieced together from old tires and ropes, but it was within those ropes that champions were forged and dreams took flight.
